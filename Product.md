# YueHaoMao Product Specification

Version 1.0

---

# Product Overview

YueHaoMao（约好猫）is a mobile-first companion selection application designed for first-time cat café visitors.

Instead of helping users simply "choose a cat", the product helps them build one enjoyable, continuous and meaningful interaction with a suitable cat before arriving at the café.

The product focuses on reducing uncertainty, lowering interaction barriers, and improving the overall first-time experience.

---

# Background

Many first-time visitors enter a cat café expecting emotional interaction.

However, the actual experience is often very different.

Some cats ignore people.

Some cats are already tired.

Some cats only respond to food.

Many visitors leave feeling disappointed, even though the café itself provides a good environment.

The problem is not the café.

The problem is the mismatch between user expectations and real interaction.

---

# Initial Observation

Different people experience completely different interaction outcomes with the same group of cats.

Some users quickly build interaction.

Others fail repeatedly.

The difference is not caused by luck.

---

# Research Question

What factors have the greatest influence on the quality of human-cat interaction in a cat café?

---

# Research Framework

The interaction experience is influenced by four dimensions.

• Person

• Cat

• Behavior

• Environment

The research focuses on identifying the dominant factor instead of optimizing every factor.

---

# Research Method

Qualitative Observation

Field Observation

Semi-structured Interviews

Affinity Mapping

Behavior Clustering

Journey Analysis

The goal is to discover behavioral patterns instead of collecting statistical data.

---

# Key Finding

Among all dimensions,

the Person dimension has the greatest influence on interaction quality.

More specifically,

Interaction outcome is mainly determined by:

Motivation × Capability

---

# Opportunity

Users with:

High Motivation

Low Capability

have the greatest opportunity for design intervention.

Why?

Because they are willing to interact,

but lack experience, confidence and effective methods.

A small amount of guidance can significantly improve their experience.

---

# Problem Statement

First-time visitors often do not know:

• Which cat is suitable for them.

• How to start interaction.

• How to keep interaction going.

As a result,

food becomes the only interaction method.

Once food is gone,

interaction usually ends.

---

# Design Insight

Current human-cat interaction inside cat cafés is often based on immediate rewards rather than emotional connection.

Food temporarily attracts cats.

But it does not help users build sustainable interaction.

The product should reduce unnecessary competition for the cat's attention,

allowing one user and one cat to spend meaningful time together.

---

# Design Goal

帮助第一次来到猫咖的人，

与一只猫真正建立一次持续的人猫互动体验。

This sentence is the highest-level design objective.

Every design decision must support this goal.

---

# Product Position

YueHaoMao is NOT

• A cat encyclopedia

• A social platform

• A pet marketplace

• A cat adoption service

• A membership system

YueHaoMao IS

A companion experience assistant.

The product exists only to improve the user's offline interaction experience.

---

# Target Users

Primary Users

First-time cat café visitors.

Characteristics

• Curious

• Emotionally motivated

• Limited interaction experience

• Unsure how to choose a suitable cat

• Hope for a memorable experience

This project does not prioritize experienced cat owners.

---

# User Needs

Functional Needs

• Choose a suitable companion cat

• Reserve interaction time

• Understand basic interaction information

Emotional Needs

• Feel welcomed

• Reduce uncertainty

• Build confidence

• Create memorable interaction

---

# Product Principles

1.

Experience before efficiency.

Helping users enjoy the interaction is more important than helping them finish tasks faster.

---

2.

Reduce decision fatigue.

Never present unnecessary information.

---

3.

Guide instead of instruct.

Offer gentle suggestions.

Never overwhelm users.

---

4.

One companion is enough.

The product encourages one meaningful interaction instead of maximizing browsing.

---

5.

Offline experience comes first.

The application supports the café experience.

It should never replace the real interaction.

---

# Success Metrics

The product succeeds if users can:

• Quickly find a suitable companion cat.

• Feel more confident before entering the café.

• Spend more continuous time interacting with one cat.

• Leave with a more satisfying first-time experience.

The product is not evaluated by feature quantity.

It is evaluated by interaction quality.

---

# Design North Star

Every feature should answer one question:

"Does this help first-time visitors build a better interaction with one cat?"

If the answer is no,

do not build it.

---

# Out of Scope

Do not redesign the product positioning.

Do not introduce unrelated business models.

Do not add features simply to make the product appear more complete.

Do not increase complexity without improving experience.

Experience is always the highest priority.
